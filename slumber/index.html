ABOUT

Slumber is a sculpture representing the sweet and precious feeling of falling into a sleep. The sculpture is soft, vulnerable, and fragile, just like any living being asleep. Its rounded form and softness invite the viewers into a warm hug and an unforgettable nap.

The sculpture intends to evoke the feeling of relaxation and replenishing of energy of body and mind during a night of sleep. Good sleep is something that we should prioritize, cherish and be excited about!

Materials

The body of the sculpture is created with tights filled with polyester filling. The stuffed tights create fascinating shapes that are incredibly soft.

The yarn ties the body and creates the head of the sculpture. A plaster lifecast of a knee was used as the face of the sculpture. The resemblance of the knee to a baby face with chubby cheeks creates an adorable look of Slumber. The minimalistic facial features reflect a calm and relaxed sleeping face. The sheer gradient scarf serves as the blanket and nicely covers the figure.
